hold

Pronunciation: /ho äld/
verb

Definition

To grasp, carry, or support with one's hands.

Example Sentences

  1. Please hold my bag while I tie my shoes.
  2. She tried to hold back her laughter.
  3. Can you hold the door open for me?

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: grasp, grip, clutch Antonyms: release, drop, let go

Etymology

From Old English 'healdan', meaning to keep or grasp; related to Old Norse 'halda'.
