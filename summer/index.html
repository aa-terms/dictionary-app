summer

Pronunciation: /ˈsʌmər/
noun

Definition

The warmest season of the year, occurring between spring and autumn.

Example Sentences

  1. We went to the beach every summer.
  2. The summer heat was intense this year.
  3. School is closed during summer vacation.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: summertime, hot season, vacation time Antonyms: winter, cold season, off-season

Etymology

From Old English 'sumor', of Germanic origin; related to Dutch 'zomer' and German 'Sommer'.
