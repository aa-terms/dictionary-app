odd

Pronunciation: /É’d/
adjective

Definition

Strange or unusual; not regular or expected.

Example Sentences

  1. He has an odd sense of humor.
  2. It's odd that she didn't call back.
  3. The socks don't match; one is odd.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: strange, peculiar, unusual Antonyms: normal, regular, even

Etymology

From Old Norse 'oddi', meaning point or triangle; later used to mean 'not paired'.
