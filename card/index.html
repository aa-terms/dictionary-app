card

Pronunciation: /kɑːrd/
noun

Definition

A piece of thick, stiff paper or thin cardboard, often used for writing or playing games.

Example Sentences

  1. She sent him a birthday card.
  2. He shuffled the card deck before dealing.
  3. Please show your ID card at the entrance.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: ticket, pass, note Antonyms: letter, envelope, sheet

Etymology

From Latin 'charta', meaning 'paper', via Old French 'carte'.
