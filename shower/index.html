shower

Pronunciation: /ˈʃaʊər/
noun, verb

Definition

A brief fall of rain; a device that produces a spray of water for washing; to bathe under a spray of water.

Example Sentences

  1. He took a quick shower before work.
  2. A light shower refreshed the garden.
  3. She likes to shower in the morning.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: rainfall, sprinkle, wash Antonyms: drought, dryness, dehydration

Etymology

From Old English 'scur', meaning 'a fall of rain'.
