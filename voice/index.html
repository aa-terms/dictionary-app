voice

Pronunciation: /vɔɪs/
noun

Definition

The sound produced in a person's larynx and uttered through the mouth; also refers to expression or opinion.

Example Sentences

  1. She raised her voice to be heard.
  2. He found his voice as a writer.
  3. The voice on the phone sounded familiar.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: speech, sound, expression Antonyms: silence, quiet, mute

Etymology

From Old French 'voiz', from Latin 'vox' meaning 'voice, sound'.
