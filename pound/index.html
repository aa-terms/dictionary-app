pound

Pronunciation: /pa änd/
noun, verb

Definition

A unit of weight equal to 16 ounces; to strike repeatedly with force.

Example Sentences

  1. He bought a pound of apples at the market.
  2. She began to pound on the door, hoping someone would hear.
  3. The drummer made the music pound through the speakers.

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms: strike, beat, hammer Antonyms: lift, caress, tap

Etymology

From Old English 'pund', from Latin 'pondo' meaning 'by weight'.
